## ⚠️ Data Disclaimer

**All data in this project is entirely fictional. StackFlow does not exist.**

Every figure in this audit — traffic numbers, keyword rankings, Domain Authority scores, conversion rates, backlink counts, competitor metrics, and projections — was invented to represent a plausible B2B SaaS scenario. No real company's data was used, accessed, or reverse-engineered.

The competitor names (Notion, Monday.com, Asana) are real products. Their approximate DA and backlink scale are directionally realistic based on publicly known industry context, but the exact figures shown are illustrative, not sourced from any SEO tool export.

Benchmarks (e.g. SaaS CVR of 3.2%, Core Web Vitals thresholds) are based on publicly available industry standards cited in the reference sources below.

This project demonstrates **SEO audit methodology, strategic prioritisation, and consulting communication** — not empirical client research.

## 🔍 Key Findings (Simulated)

- Organic traffic down 34% YoY — linked to 14 lost page-1 rankings after Google's March 2024 core update
- Conversion rate at 1.8% vs SaaS benchmark of 3.2% — 3 friction points identified in sign-up flow
- Domain Authority at 28 vs competitors at 76–81 — backlink gap is the structural constraint
- Top quick win: internal linking overhaul — 47 orphaned pages identified, 1-week fix, high ranking impact

## 📚 Reference Sources

The audit methodology, benchmarks, and scoring criteria were informed by the following publicly available industry sources.

- **Ahrefs Blog** — *SEO Audit: How to Do a Complete Site Audit*. Methodology reference for technical SEO audit structure (Core Web Vitals, internal linking, crawlability). [ahrefs.com/blog](https://ahrefs.com/blog/seo-audit/)
- **Semrush** — *State of Search 2024*. Keyword difficulty scoring methodology and SERP feature benchmarks. [semrush.com](https://www.semrush.com/state-of-search/)
- **Google Search Central** — *Core Web Vitals*. Official thresholds for LCP (<2.5s), FID (<100ms), CLS (<0.1) used in technical recommendations. [developers.google.com](https://developers.google.com/search/docs/appearance/core-web-vitals)
- **Wordstream / LocaliQ** — *Google Ads Industry Benchmarks 2024*. CPC and conversion rate benchmarks for B2B SaaS paid search. [wordstream.com](https://www.wordstream.com/blog/ws/2016/02/29/google-adwords-industry-benchmarks)
- **First Page Sage** — *SaaS Conversion Rate Benchmarks 2024*. Trial-to-paid and free trial sign-up CVR norms (benchmark: 3.0–3.5% for B2B SaaS). [firstpagesage.com](https://firstpagesage.com/seo-blog/saas-conversion-rate/)
- **Moz** — *Domain Authority explanation and competitive benchmarking methodology*. [moz.com/learn/seo/domain-authority](https://moz.com/learn/seo/domain-authority)
- **HubSpot** — *Marketing Statistics 2024*. Channel mix benchmarks (organic vs paid vs direct split for SaaS). [hubspot.com/marketing-statistics](https://www.hubspot.com/marketing-statistics)
